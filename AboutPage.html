About Us

We are the world's largest university

The Bharathiyar University is a State university located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in February 1982, it is named after the great revolutionary Tamil poet, Subramania Bharathiyar. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission and accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade. The university is a residential cum teaching one with a campus of about 100 acres. The university has 38 departments, 4 post graduate research centers, 1 constituent college and 3 autonomous colleges offering more than 150 programs.
